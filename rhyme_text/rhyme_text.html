One of the most valued Links traditions is writing rhymes. Please create a rhyme about what being a member in Links would mean to you. Length may be no shorter than four lines and no longer than half a page.

Shall I encapsulate my deep desire
To don a rugby worn by Links of past?
Nay, for my love extends beyond attire
And celebrates traditions well amass'd.
For if I were to be a Link next year,
In J.B.S.'s legacy I'd dwell,
Through service, love, philanthropy, and cheer,
To be a Link shall influence me well.
Dear Bigs, it's time to pass the torch along,
And mayhap thou shall pass it on to me,
For I shall keep our values ever strong
And wear my rugby proud with joy and glee.

Much like the chain, we're linked in friendship true,

And I'll live, laugh, love, Links, with all I do.
